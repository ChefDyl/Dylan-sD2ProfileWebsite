ABOUT THE TITAN

Out of the three classes in Destiny 2, Warlocks are most well known for their tanking and defensive capabilities. Titans, while they lack the same agility as the other two classes (most of the time), they generally boast the highest resilience stats out of the three classes, making them very tanky. They are also capable of supporting their team by placing down cover at a moment's notice. They are also quite capable of outputting some large damage as well.

In the current sandbox, Titans are currently the strongest class in PVP due to their defensive abilties that are so strong they alone are capable of winning winning entire games. Titans are the "tank" class equivalent of Destiny 2, the gameplay being all about defensive play, and being the steadfast wall for your teammates in the face of danger. They also really, really like to punch things.
